Job Title: Data Analyst
Company: Northwind Retail Analytics
Location: Remote (US)

About the Role:
We are seeking a Data Analyst to join our Business Intelligence team. You will work
closely with marketing, operations, and finance stakeholders to turn raw data into
actionable insights. The ideal candidate has 3+ years of experience working with SQL,
Excel, and BI tools, and is comfortable presenting findings to non-technical audiences.

Responsibilities:
- Write and optimize SQL queries against large relational databases
- Build and maintain dashboards in Tableau and Power BI
- Perform exploratory data analysis using Python (Pandas, NumPy) or R
- Partner with stakeholders to define KPIs and reporting requirements
- Conduct A/B testing and statistical analysis to support product decisions
- Clean and validate datasets from multiple sources (ETL pipelines)
- Present findings and recommendations to leadership

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Economics, Computer Science, or related field
- 3+ years of experience in a data analyst or business intelligence role
- Strong proficiency in SQL and Excel
- Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI
- Working knowledge of Python or R for data analysis
- Familiarity with statistical analysis and A/B testing methodology
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Strong attention to detail and analytical thinking

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Google Analytics or Amplitude
- Exposure to data warehousing concepts (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift)
- Experience with dbt or other ETL/ELT tools
- Master's degree in a quantitative field

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and equity
- Remote-first culture with flexible hours
- Learning and development budget
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
